What's ClickCrystals?

ClickCrystals is a combination of many useful crystal pvp features, while supported a more advanced hotkey system. Instead of pressing keys for hotkeys, you interact with items in-game!

Why ClickCrystals?

The ClickCrystals Project is a free and open-source Minecraft client modification that allows all in one features without you having to download a lot of mods. The project also hosts an friend and active community that is constantly giving new ideas! Be sure to join!

ClickCrystals is also highly customizable with over 40 modules, each with a unqiue set of settings to customize your user experience.

How do I download?

Just click on the download link on the navigation bar on the top of this screen! Or you can head over to Modrinth and get the mod there.

Do keep in mind that the only acceptable site get download this mod from is Modrinth! Please refrain from getting the mod through direct messages or other websites like curseforge, as those sites do not check for malicous software; You might be ratted!
